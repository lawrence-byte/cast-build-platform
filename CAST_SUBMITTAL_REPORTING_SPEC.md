# CAST Submittal Reporting Spec

Reports: Open Submittal Log, Closed Submittal Log, Overdue Submittal Log, Submittal Aging Report, Ball in Court Report, Submittals by Responsible Contractor, Submittals by Spec Section, Submittals by Package, Submittals by Type, Submittals with Schedule Impact, Submittals with Cost Impact, Approver Response Time Report, Submittal Manager Workload Report, Closeout Submittal Report, Revision History Report.

All reports are filterable and exportable to CSV in MVP. PDF export is scaffolded through browser print/export behavior for future backend rendering.
