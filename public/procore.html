Connect CAST BUILD A.O as the construction data backbone while keeping CAST Build’s own executive workspace, reporting, workflow rules, and knowledge layer.

Workflow coverage

Project controls

Projects, users, roles, companies, locations, cost codes, schedules.

Read model: normalize source IDs, statuses, responsible parties, due dates, linked records, and source timestamps.

Field + documents

Drawings, specs, documents, photos, daily logs, observations, punch list.

Read model: support open-item reports, current-set checks, document context, field packet summaries, and closeout readiness.

Financials

Budget, commitments, change events, change orders, invoices, direct costs.

Read model: separate actual export values from local planning scenarios and keep source write-back disabled.
