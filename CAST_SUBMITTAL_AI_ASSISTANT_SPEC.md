# CAST Submittal AI Assistant Spec

AI suggestions can draft descriptions, identify missing information/attachments, suggest spec/drawing references, summarize review history/comments, flag conflicting responses, identify schedule/cost impact, generate weekly summaries, overdue lists by contractor, and closeout readiness summaries.

AI cannot approve, mark official, submit responses, close, revise, or send notifications without explicit user approval. Suggestions remain separate from official records until accepted.
